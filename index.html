Our Story

Welcome to The Purple PH, a vibrant tapestry of style and entrepreneurship woven together by the passion of our founder, Cindy Rusiel. What began as a means to earn extra income during her college days has blossomed into a thriving journey, culminating in the colorful story of The Purple PH.

A Journey from College to Couture

Cindy Rusiel's entrepreneurial spirit ignited during her college days when she started selling Ready-to-Wear (RTW) clothing. Driven by the desire to be her own boss and fueled by the love for business, she turned this humble venture into a canvas for her creative and entrepreneurial aspirations.

Painting the Digital Canvas

In pursuit of her dreams, Cindy successfully launched The Purple PH as an online store on Instagram. This digital space became a haven for fashion enthusiasts and purple lovers alike. The carefully curated collection of clothing and accessories echoed Cindy's dedication to quality and style, captivating an ever-growing audience.

Expanding Horizons

The year 2017 marked a pivotal moment as The Purple PH expanded its footprint, joining prominent online shopping platforms like Shopee and Lazada. This move not only broadened our reach but also brought the magic of purple to a diverse community, fostering connections with individuals who share our passion for style and entrepreneurship.

Bridging the Gap: The Physical Store

In 2022, The Purple PH took a bold leap into the physical realm with the opening of our first brick-and-mortar store in Bulakan. This transition from the digital space to a tangible store symbolized not just a business milestone but also a commitment to providing an immersive and personal shopping experience for our cherished customers.

Blooming in Bulacan: Second Branch

The year 2023 heralds a new chapter for The Purple PH, as we proudly announce the opening of our second branch in Guiguinto, Bulacan. This expansion reflects the continued growth of The Purple PH family and our dedication to bringing the beauty of purple fashion to even more enthusiasts.
